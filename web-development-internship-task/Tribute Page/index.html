Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

1630 – 1680

"शत्रू कितीही बलाढ्य असला तरी धैर्य आणि बुद्धीने विजय मिळवता येतो."

Biography

Chhatrapati Shivaji Shahaji Bhosale was the founder of the Maratha Empire and one of the greatest warriors and administrators in Indian history. He established a progressive and people-centric rule based on justice, courage, and self-governance.

Major Achievements

  • Founded the Maratha Empire
  • Developed a strong naval force
  • Implemented efficient administrative reforms
  • Promoted religious tolerance
